# Content Legal Guard

## Purpose

Keep public wording discreet, premium, and legally careful.

## Avoid

- direct public offer language
- public price language
- guaranteed availability
- guaranteed charter terms
- “book now”
- “rent this yacht now”
- legally binding promises
- ownership details
- owner identity
- operational claims not verified

## Prefer

- private presentation
- by request
- selected dates
- subject to confirmation
- inquiry
- introduction
- guest experience
- private arrangement
- broker conversation
- availability discussed privately

## Review Output

For any copy, provide:

1. Risky phrases
2. Safer replacements
3. Premium wording option
4. Notes for legal review if needed
